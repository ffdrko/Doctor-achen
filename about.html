About


Doctor Achen is a convenient way to look for doctors nearby. This web app promises to get in touch with a medical professional in times of emergency. A patient can look out for doctors near them, book an appointment, and know which facilities are available in centers or clinics. They will also get the facility of managing medical history, getting diagnostics reports & prescriptions. We have our verification system to select the most qualified doctors in order to provide optimum medical services. The doctors can consult only after getting verified. Also, there is a doctor rating system for patients after every consultation to monitor their service. With the current doctor-patient ratio (only 5.26 doctors per 10,000 population), it is not easy to get proper treatment in Bangladesh. People find it very challenging to look for the doctors available. For this hassle, they are prone to treating by themselves. Also, there are plenty of fraudulent services provided by unqualified doctors. Using our web application, we wish to stop whimsical medical practices by common people and encourage them to consult a professional even for trivial matters
